Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response?

Our team strives to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 business hours. During peak seasons, response times may vary, but we prioritize urgent requests.

Can I schedule a garden consultation online?

Yes! Contact us through this form or by phone to schedule a personalized garden consultation. We'll coordinate with you to find a convenient time.

What are your service areas?

Currently, we provide services primarily within New York City and surrounding boroughs. For locations outside, please inquire for availability.

Do you offer emergency services?

We understand emergencies happen and offer limited emergency garden support. Please call us directly for urgent requests to check availability.

How can I update or cancel a scheduled service?

For updates or cancellations, please contact our customer support promptly by phone or email. Early notice helps us serve you better.

Helpful Tips for Contacting Us

To help us assist you effectively, please provide clear and detailed information in your messages, including:

  • Your full name and contact details
  • Preferred contact method and best times to reach you
  • Details about your garden or service needs
  • Any specific questions or concerns
  • If applicable, photos or descriptions of your garden or issues

Providing these details will enable us to respond promptly and tailor our services to your needs.

Privacy and Security of Your Information

We respect your privacy and handle your personal data securely as detailed in our Privacy Policy. Your contact information is used solely for responding to your inquiries and improving our services.
